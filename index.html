Weekly Exercise Habit Tracker

Creating a habit tracker is an incredibly effective way to turn your goals into reality. By visually tracking your progress, you stay motivated and committed, as each box serves as a small victory that encourages you to keep going.

Track your daily exercises for the week. Check off each day you complete the habit. Progress and streak will update automatically. Streak counts consecutive days from Monday onwards.
